######################################
Axon PCIe to Ethernet Expension Board
######################################

.. image:: /_static/images/rk3588-axon/axon-hat-ethernet.webp
   :width: 45%
   :align: center

Axon Ethernet Hat is an accessory designed to provide Ethernet connectivity to Axon. It allows you to connect your Axon SBC to a wired network, enabling faster and more stable internet connections for your projects.

.. note::

   Connect Expansion board while Axon is powered off to avoid any damage and PCIe enumeration can only be done during bootup, it not hot-pluggable.

Specifications
---------------

- Connector: Dual 2.5GbE RJ45 Ethernet ports
- Interface: PCIe 2.0 (×2 lanes)
- FPC Cable: 30 Pin

Applications:
--------------

- Edge computing
- Custom firewalls and VPNs
- Industrial network gateways
- High-speed NAS and file servers
- Smart home hubs and routers
